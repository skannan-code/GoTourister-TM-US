# GoTourister-TM-US
## 😁Features
GoTourister is a website built for explorers and adventurers ready to step out of their comfort zone. It displays multiple inbuilt options that take you to different pages in the same website that talk about places you can explore.
The best part? It's all for free and contains open-source code from HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
## 🔭Extras to Explore
Normally, websites are boring and unappealing to the eyes of explorers. You may be asking yourself, why is GoTourister any different? I'll tell you why. Here at GoTourister, we have a section dedicated to an in-built program called "HotelTracker". HotelTracker shows you places you can stay on your visit based on your budget. It has 4 different budgeting levels. So, it works for everyone!
